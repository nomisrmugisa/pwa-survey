13.2 Linen Service Management
13.2.1 Standard
The linen service is managed to ensure the provision of a safe and effective
service.
Standard Intent: Linen management encompasses all aspects of the provision of clean linen for
all patient care services. The laundry service may be provided on site or off site.  Whatever
system is used, the processes will be assessed in terms of the provision and distribution of
linen, stock control, the collection of soiled and infected linen, laundering processes and the
redistribution of linen.
  
Departmental and service managers are primarily responsible for ensuring that the mission
of the organisation is met through the provision of management and leadership at
departmental level. Good departmental or service performances require clear leadership
from a suitably qualified individual.
